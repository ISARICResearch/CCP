Site Capacity: Points to Consider Check List

The competencies/skills presented are by no means an extensive list of staff competencies to carry out the CCP.

For a more in depth assessment, consider exploring the Global Health Network’s Professional Development Scheme, visit https://globalhealthtrainingcentre.tghn.org/pds/core-competencyframework/’.

Text highlighted in pink is a guide to better practice1. In all instances you must ensure that you adhere to local and national guidelines.
